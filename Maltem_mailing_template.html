September 2018 Edition
 
On the 30th of May, the 3rd session of Think-Tank SENS.Club was organized in Singapore alongside Maltem Asia's team, and graced by the presence of the Ambassador of France to Singapore. The impact of the digital revolution on education and training was the main subject of debate. The event gathered an international panel of high-level speakers, such as Robert Kauffman, Xavier Pavie, Lim Chee Koon, Pierre-Etienne Pommier, Hadrian Duzan, Yoni Garburg, Hemlani Lavine and Romain Pigenel, bringing together university professors, academics and start-up entrepreneurs. They agreed on the increasing importance of the human factors, and the need to rethink the digital solution with a strategic vision, and serving the people first.
